This article is a landing page for those interested in the **[ppc]** and **[ppc64]** architectures.

PPC and PPC64 historically includes [Power Macintosh](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Power_Macintosh "wikipedia:Power Macintosh") computers and, in modern times, mostly consists of machines running [IBM Power microprocessors](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IBM_Power_microprocessors "wikipedia:IBM Power microprocessors") on a [Power ISA](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Power_ISA "wikipedia:Power ISA"). Power ISAS presently developed by the [OpenPOWER Foundation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OpenPOWER_Foundation "wikipedia:OpenPOWER Foundation").
