**Winetricks** is an easy way to work around problems in [Wine](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Wine "Wine").

It is a [Shell](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Shell "Shell") script allowing for easy installation of applications, DLLs, fonts, workarounds, and more.
