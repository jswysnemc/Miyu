Presenting news as it developed, the news project was intended to provide the Gentoo community a stream of information regarding changes in the Gentoo universe.

Preceding the news project, the Gentoo Monthly Newsletter (GMN) summarized the key events in the Gentoo Linux project every month and presented them in a concise format. The news project was a brief, direct successor to (GMN), which, in turn, succeeded the Gentoo Weekly Newsletter (GWN) project before it. Now news is primary handled by the [website project](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Project:Website "Project:Website"), the [Public relations team](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Project:Public_Relations "Project:Public Relations"), and various Gentoo developer blogs.
