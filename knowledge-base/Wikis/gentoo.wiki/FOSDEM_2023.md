On 2023-02-04 and 2023-02-05, the Free and Open Source Software Developers\' European Meeting [FOSDEM 2023](https://fosdem.org/2023/) will take place in Brussels, Belgium.

Naturally a Gentoo crowd will be there; the Gentoo stand has been confirmed in the meantime.

This page will collect public information about our presence, and help with organizing stuff.

## [Gentoo dinner and other social events]

We recommend you join [[#gentoo-fosdem](ircs://irc.libera.chat/#gentoo-fosdem)] ([[webchat](https://web.libera.chat/#gentoo-fosdem)]) for last minute notices.

### [Friday]

Unlike previous years, [there will be no official beer event on Friday](https://fosdem.org/2023/practical/covid/). It is not known yet if an unofficial event will be held instead.

### [Saturday]

We have a reservation for 20 persons, 20:30, at the [Greenwich Modern](https://greenwichmodern.be/en), Kartuizersstraat 7 1000 Brussel, België ([Google maps link](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Greenwich+Caf%C3%A9+Bruxelles/@50.8493176,4.3478462,14z/data=!4m5!3m4!1s0x0:0x4f3464e7a451b09d!8m2!3d50.8489465!4d4.3471854)). See you there!
