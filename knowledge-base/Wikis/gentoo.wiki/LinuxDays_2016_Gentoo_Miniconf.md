On Saturday 8 October 2016 and Sunday 9 October 2016, the [LinuxDays 2016](https://www.linuxdays.cz/2016/) and the [Gentoo Miniconf](https://blogs.gentoo.org/miniconf-2016) will take place in Prague, Czech Republic.

This page is intended to collect public information about our presence and help organizing stuff.
