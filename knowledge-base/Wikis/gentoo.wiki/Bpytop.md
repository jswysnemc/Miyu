**bpytop** is a resource monitor that shows usage and stats for processor, memory, disks, network and processes. It is a continuation of [bashtop](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Bashtop "Bashtop") and succeeded by [btop](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Btop "Btop").

## [Installation]

### [Emerge]

`root `[`#`]`emerge --ask sys-process/bpytop`

## [Usage]

To start bpytop, you can run

`user `[`$`]`bpytop`
