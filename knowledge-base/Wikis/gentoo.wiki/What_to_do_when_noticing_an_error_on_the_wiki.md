Spot a mistake, omission, broken link, etc.? Please [just fix it], it\'s easy\![ ]

Making a small change is simple: [once logged-in](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Special:CreateAccount "Special:CreateAccount"), click the *Edit* button at the top of a page or next to a section title, make the appropriate change to the text, add a brief summary stating *what* the edit changes and *why*, click the *Save changes* button - done!\

------------------------------------------------------------------------

For useful information on editing the wiki, see the quick-start guide on [what to do when noticing an error on the wiki‎], the [contributor\'s guide](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Contributor%27s_guide "Gentoo Wiki:Contributor's guide"), and the [guidelines](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Guidelines "Gentoo Wiki:Guidelines").

For the *occasional quick fix*, don\'t necessarily worry too much about [formatting and style](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Contributor%27s_guide "Gentoo Wiki:Contributor's guide") if it\'s too much - what\'s important is to fix errors or add missing information to the wiki.

If having any trouble getting started, there are often people willing to help in the [[#gentoo-wiki](ircs://irc.libera.chat/#gentoo-wiki)] ([[webchat](https://web.libera.chat/#gentoo-wiki)]) [IRC](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/IRC "IRC") channel. Please be patient, replies can take a very long time: be prepared to wait for several hours after asking a question.

Note that some articles are protected from public edits, such as the [handbook](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Handbook:Main_Page "Handbook:Main Page") and [project pages](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Project:Gentoo "Project:Gentoo"). Issues on these pages can still be [reported](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/What_to_do_when_noticing_an_error_on_the_wiki#Inform_editors_of_issues_on_protected_pages_and_propose_solutions "What to do when noticing an error on the wiki") though.

For anyone willing to help the Gentoo community out a little more than with the occasional quick fix, please do check out the [contributor\'s guide](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Contributor%27s_guide "Gentoo Wiki:Contributor's guide") and the [wiki editing guidelines](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Guidelines "Gentoo Wiki:Guidelines") to learn how to make quality edits. The [help improve Gentoo by getting involved with documentation!](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Help_improve_Gentoo_by_getting_involved_with_documentation! "Help improve Gentoo by getting involved with documentation!") page lists many of the ways wiki content can be improved.

There may occasionally be errors on the wiki, as the volume of documentation managed here means some things will inevitably slip through. Anyone reading the wiki who comes across an error can help fix things though! Here are instructions on **what to do when noticing an error on the wiki**, to get things fixed.

** Tip**\
Please use the [just fix it](#Just_fix_it) option, if at all possible. It\'s quick, and means the least work for an already overstretched team ;).

** Note**\
Some pages are protected and not publicly editable, and will have a \"view source\" button in place of the \"Edit\" buttons. For these pages, please [flag issues on the relevant talk page](#Inform_editors_of_issues_on_protected_pages_and_propose_solutions).

## [Just fix it]

The most useful thing that can be done about an issue on the wiki is to [create an account](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Special:CreateAccount "Special:CreateAccount"), if not already done, and just fix the issue directly on the wiki! This is the best thing anyone can do to help out around here. Don\'t worry too much about formatting, or the quality of the English - editors can touch this up later - the main thing is to get erroneous information removed from the wiki.

If an edit is short, and makes things better than they were before, it should be a good contribution, at least to work from. Just refrain from making large edits, as these can be hard to untangle.

Check out the [contributor\'s guide](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Contributor%27s_guide "Gentoo Wiki:Contributor's guide") for more information on creating accounts, on how to start editing the wiki, and on how to make the best additions possible. If making more substantial edits to the wiki, please do be sure to check out that guide, and the [official wiki guidelines](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Guidelines "Gentoo Wiki:Guidelines").

## [Flag the issue on talk pages]

If a solution to a problem found in an article isn\'t immediately apparent, please do still leave a note on the offending article\'s [talk page](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Help:Talk_pages "Help:Talk pages") to inform everyone of the issue. This way another editor can come along and fix it, or the team can ask for follow-up information on what is wrong.

## [Inform editors of issues on protected pages and propose solutions]

Pages such as the [handbook](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Handbook:Main_Page "Handbook:Main Page") and [project pages](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Project:Gentoo "Project:Gentoo") have editing restricted to try to ensure quality.

Any changes needed to these [protected pages](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Help:Protected_pages "Help:Protected pages") should be added to a new section of the relevant [talk page](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Help:Talk_pages "Help:Talk pages"). If possible, please do propose a solution to any issues with the [}](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Template:Proposal "Template:Proposal") template:

It is very useful when flagging issues on protected pages to propose a fix on the corresponding talk page with the **[}](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Template:Proposal "Template:Proposal") template.** This can both make things clearer as to what the issue is, and save time for the editors.

## [If all else fails]

There are always other ways to help get an issue fixed, so here are a couple for reference, but please do just fix things directly or use the talk pages if a discussion is really needed.

### [][IRC in #gentoo-wiki]

Though the above solutions are preferable, it is possible to come and tell us on [IRC](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/IRC "IRC") about an issue, on the [[#gentoo-wiki](ircs://irc.libera.chat/#gentoo-wiki)] ([[webchat](https://web.libera.chat/#gentoo-wiki)]) channel. Please only do this as a final recourse, as it is much easier for the wiki team to organize their work when things are clearly stated on talk pages, and this avoids things being forgotten or missed (not everybody has time to constantly monitor the IRC channel).

### [Contact wiki team by email]

If there really isn\'t any other way, it\'s always possible to [get in touch with a wiki administrator](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:FAQ#How_to_contact_the_wiki_admins "Gentoo Wiki:FAQ"), for example by email.
