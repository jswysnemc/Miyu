**Falkon** (formerly QupZilla) is a lightweight web browser based on QtWebEngine. Being quite lean, Falkon is visually attractive and functional. It excellently integrates into [desktop environments](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Desktop_environment "Desktop environment") and looks good on [KDE](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/KDE "KDE") and [LXQt](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/LXQt "LXQt").
