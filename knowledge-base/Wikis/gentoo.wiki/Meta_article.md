A **meta article** is an article that provides a general overview or summary of conceptually similar ideas and related software available in Gentoo Linux.

## [Meta article categories]

A [Meta article category](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Category:Meta "Category:Meta") is available for tagging meta-style articles.
