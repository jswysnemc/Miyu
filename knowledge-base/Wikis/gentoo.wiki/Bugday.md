Bugday is a day devoted to development efforts pertaining to a certain area of the Gentoo project, organized at most one a month. See [GLEP 6](https://www.gentoo.org/glep/glep-0006.html) for more information on bug day\'s origin.

**Next Bugday: [-]**

**Bugday focus: [-]**

**Who:** Gentoo community (developers and users) - organized by the [Bug Wranglers](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Project:Bug-wranglers "Project:Bug-wranglers") project.

**When:** On the first **Saturday** of the month, when applicable.

**Time:** 24h event (Gentoo is world wide)!

**Meeting:** The IRC channel [[#gentoo-bugday](ircs://irc.libera.chat/#gentoo-bugday)] ([[webchat](https://web.libera.chat/#gentoo-bugday)]) on Libera.Chat.

## [][How to prepare (highly suggested)]

1.  [Create a Gentoo Bugzilla account](https://bugs.gentoo.org/createaccount.cgi).
2.  [Create a Gentoo wiki account](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Special:CreateAccount "Special:CreateAccount").
3.  Setup an [IRC client](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/IRC "IRC") (join [[#gentoo-bugday](ircs://irc.libera.chat/#gentoo-bugday)] ([[webchat](https://web.libera.chat/#gentoo-bugday)]). See also our [IRC channels](https://www.gentoo.org/get-involved/irc-channels/): [[#gentoo](ircs://irc.libera.chat/#gentoo)] ([[webchat](https://web.libera.chat/#gentoo)]), [[#gentoo-dev-help](ircs://irc.libera.chat/#gentoo-dev-help)] ([[webchat](https://web.libera.chat/#gentoo-dev-help)]), [[#gentoo-proxy-maint](ircs://irc.libera.chat/#gentoo-proxy-maint)] ([[webchat](https://web.libera.chat/#gentoo-proxy-maint)])).
4.  Read our [how to contribute](https://www.gentoo.org/get-involved/contribute/) page.
