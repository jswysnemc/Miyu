The **m68k** architecture is supported within Gentoo through the [Gentoo m68k Project](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Project:M68k "Project:M68k").

### [Supported Stages]

Currently Gentoo supports four stage3 tarballs:

-   OpenRC with Glibc
-   OpenRC with Musl
-   Systemd with or without mergeusr

All stages can found [here](http://distfiles.gentoo.org/releases/m68k/autobuilds/).
