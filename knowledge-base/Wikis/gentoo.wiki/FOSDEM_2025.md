On 2025-02-01 and 2025-02-02, the Free and Open Source Software Developers\' European Meeting [FOSDEM 2025](https://fosdem.org/2025/) will take place in Brussels, Belgium.

Naturally a Gentoo crowd will be there. This year, we have a joint stand together with [Flatcar Container Linux](https://www.flatcar.org/).

Sadly, (at least as far as I know) for yet another year there will be no official Beer Event.

## [Gentoo dinner and other social events]

We recommend you join [[#gentoo-fosdem](ircs://irc.libera.chat/#gentoo-fosdem)] ([[webchat](https://web.libera.chat/#gentoo-fosdem)]) for last minute notices.

### [Friday]

There will be no official beer event on Friday. It is not known yet if an unofficial event will be held instead.

### [Saturday]

There will be a **Gentoo dinner on Saturday evening, 19:30**, at Cafe Le Cirio, Rue de la Bourse 18, 1000 Bruxelles ([homepage](https://lecirio.be/en)); we have reserved a table for 20 persons under the name Huttel.
