Croaker is an IRC bot that operates as a doom herald to Gentoo developers who have contributed a change to the ::gentoo ebuild repository that has not passed QA checks.

Croaker lurks in the [[#gentoo-dev](ircs://irc.libera.chat/#gentoo-dev)] ([[webchat](https://web.libera.chat/#gentoo-dev)]) channel. Its source is part of the [repo-mirror-ci](https://github.com/projg2/repo-mirror-ci) code which is operated by [Michał Górny (mgorny)](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/User:MGorny "User:MGorny") .

## [Usage]

The bot writes QA messages, but does not interact with users.
