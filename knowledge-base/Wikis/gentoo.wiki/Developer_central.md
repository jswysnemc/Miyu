### [  Developer central ]

This page is a landing that provides resources for official Gentoo developers. Official developers have a gentoo.org email address.

All developers should link their Gentoo Infra LDAP record to their developer account here on the wiki:

[ Link a developer account](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Developer_Central/Getting_started "Gentoo Wiki:Developer Central/Getting started")

New projects can be quickly created on the wiki via the Project form:

[ Create a project new page](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Developer_Central/Project_pages "Gentoo Wiki:Developer Central/Project pages")

File hosting for things that will not fit on the wiki (code, meeting notes, etc.) is provided via [projects.g.o](https://projects.gentoo.org/):

[ Project file hosting](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Project:Infrastructure/Project_file_hosting "Project:Infrastructure/Project file hosting")

### [ Getting started]

The wiki has the [contributor\'s guide](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Contributor%27s_guide "Gentoo Wiki:Contributor's guide") for getting started, as well as its own extensive [help pages](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Help:Contents "Help:Contents") for learning more about syntax and formatting and a [Guidelines](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Guidelines "Gentoo Wiki:Guidelines") article that outlines the formatting standards for our wiki. Those who want to take a deep dive on how MediaWiki software works can swim upstream to the [MediaWiki User\'s Guide](https://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/Help:Contents).

For any questions *related to the wiki*, join the [[#gentoo-wiki](ircs://irc.libera.chat/#gentoo-wiki)] ([[webchat](https://web.libera.chat/#gentoo-wiki)]) IRC channel, or send an email to [wiki@gentoo.org](mailto:wiki@gentoo.org). If there is something to improve, please let us know on the [Feedback page](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Feedback "Gentoo Wiki:Feedback")!

### [  News ]

#### [][August 04, 2022: More updates to developer and project wiki forms]

Wiki admins would like to formally announce more quality of life enhancements to developer and project \'pages\' here on the wiki. Enhancements include:

-   Links to related bugs on [bugs.g.o](https://bugzilla.gentoo.org) can now be activated on Gentoo project boxes (closes [[[bug #686264]](https://bugs.gentoo.org/show_bug.cgi?id=686264)[]]. Individual projects can opt-in with the checkbox on each respective project page.
-   A new field for developers to post a \'fund me\' link directly on their developer \'profile\'. None of our developers are paid by the Gentoo Foundation; the vast majority of our devs freely volunteer their time to maintain and improve Gentoo. Please consider donating to our devs who have listed their fund me info.
