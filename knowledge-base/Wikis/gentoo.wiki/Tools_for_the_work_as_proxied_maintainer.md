### [][Compare and merge ebuilds (diff)]

### [Testing ebuilds]

Every proxied maintainer has to [test packages](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Package_testing "Package testing") before committing them. A [test environment](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Test_environment "Test environment") can be useful.
