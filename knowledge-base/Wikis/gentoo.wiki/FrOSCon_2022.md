### [Gentoo Stand]

We will have a stand at FrOSCon on both days. Everything is being prepared!

What we have there:

-   All kind of Gentoo swag like t-shirts, mugs
-   Tux riding Larry to freedom
-   Sweets (We have a secret contract with the local dentists!)
-   The button press machine
    -   Now with one gentoonftcoin per button!
-   Much fun and interesting talks.
-   A GNU Slush Linux machine (Water ice with flavour machine)
-   Over 2400 blinking LEDs, so you can easily find us at night.
-   (What will you bring with you?)
