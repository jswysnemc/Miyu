This is a general grouping of things needing work around the wiki. Anybody starting out contributing to the wiki may find articles from here that require work, such as in the [todo articles](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Category:Todo_articles "Category:Todo articles") subcategory or in the [stub list](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Category:Stub "Category:Stub").

After having read the [wiki guidelines](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Guidelines "Gentoo Wiki:Guidelines") and [contributor\'s guide](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Contributor%27s_guide "Gentoo Wiki:Contributor's guide"), many easy edits can be found in [Category:Wikify articles](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Category:Wikify_articles "Category:Wikify articles") and [Category:Dirty](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Category:Dirty "Category:Dirty"), simply to adjust articles to conform to the wiki formatting and style.

See [help improve Gentoo by getting involved with documentation!](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Help_improve_Gentoo_by_getting_involved_with_documentation! "Help improve Gentoo by getting involved with documentation!") for things that regularly need looking out for on the wiki.

Anyone who has the knowledge to tackle edits in the other Todo subcategories is of course welcome to do so.

There is also a list of [requested articles](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Requested_Articles "Gentoo Wiki:Requested Articles"), and of [inexistant pages that have links](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Special:WantedPages "Special:WantedPages") to them.

[Gentoo\'s bugzilla](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Bugzilla/Guide "Bugzilla/Guide") has a [list of issues](https://bugs.gentoo.org/buglist.cgi?component=Wiki&list_id=7017912&order=bug_id+DESC&product=Websites&query_format=advanced&resolution=---) about the wiki that editors may sometimes be able to help with. The [documentation component bug list](https://bugs.gentoo.org/buglist.cgi?bug_status=__open__&list_id=7017955&order=changeddate+DESC%2Cpriority%2Cbug_severity&product=Documentation&query_format=specific) may sometimes also contain items of interest.

** Note**\
Do not confuse this general Todo category with [Category:Todo articles](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Category:Todo_articles "Category:Todo articles"), which are articles which have Todo items indicated by the [}](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Template:Todo "Template:Todo") or [}](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Template:InfoBox_todo "Template:InfoBox todo") templates.
