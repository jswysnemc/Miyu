This is a list of **ARM hardware** owned by people in the [[#gentoo-embedded](ircs://irc.libera.chat/#gentoo-embedded)] ([[webchat](https://web.libera.chat/#gentoo-embedded)]) and [[#gentoo-arm](ircs://irc.libera.chat/#gentoo-arm)] ([[webchat](https://web.libera.chat/#gentoo-arm)]) channels have running Gentoo. Feel free to add to the list!

The [hardware probe](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Hardware_probe "Hardware probe") system may be used to provide detailed hardware information, by uploading a probe using the available tool, and providing a link to that probe along with the entry here (see entry by *Jens3* for an example).
