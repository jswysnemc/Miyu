Foliate is a free and open-source eBook reader that supports EPUB, Mobipocket, Kindle, FictionBook, and comic book archive formats.

It comes with multiple features including but not limited to highlighting, note-taking, and multiple themes (including gruvbox, sepia, and solarized).
