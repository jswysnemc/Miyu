**Sandbox** could have a variety of meanings:

-   [Gentoo Wiki:Sandbox](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Sandbox "Gentoo Wiki:Sandbox") - A \"public\" Gentoo Wiki testing area.
-   [Sandbox (Portage)](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Sandbox_(Portage) "Sandbox (Portage)") - A Portage tool for Gentoo development, and associated [project](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Project:Sandbox "Project:Sandbox").
-   [Simple sandbox](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Simple_sandbox "Simple sandbox") article - Virtual containers to secure applications.
