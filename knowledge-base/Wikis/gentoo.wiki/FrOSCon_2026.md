### [Gentoo booth]

In 2024 and 2025 we had a devroom. In 2026 we would like to organize a booth again and provide buttons and shirts. Gentoo related talks at the FrOSCon are also very welcome. We need helping hands to realize the booth. Contact vorstand at gentoo-ev.org if you want to help with the booth.
