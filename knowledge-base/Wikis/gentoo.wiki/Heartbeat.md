**Heartbeat** is a deprected cluster messaging layer that was historically used with [Pacemaker](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Pacemaker "Pacemaker"). Although it is available in [portage](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Portage "Portage"), today [Corosync](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Corosync "Corosync") is the preferred messaging layer and Heartbeat is not recommended for new deployments.
