**The Fediverse** is a federated and decentralized worldwide Internet-based social network built upon the [ActivityPub](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ActivityPub "wikipedia:ActivityPub") protocol. Social network platforms that federate over the ActivityPub protocol are said to be \"part of the Fediverse.\" Most social networks in the Fediverse natively run the ActivityPub protocol but some choose to implement their own protocol internally but implement, to one extent or another, an ActivityPub gateway to pass messages to and from the wider Fediverse.

Major Fediverse communities include:

-   Mastodon and Pleroma --- micro blogging akin to Twitter (now called \"X\").
-   Lemmy --- content aggregation and discussion akin to Reddit and [Usenet](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Usenet "Usenet").
-   Write Freely --- long form blogging akin to WordPress.
-   Diaspora\* --- a Facebook analog that partially federates with the Fediverse.
