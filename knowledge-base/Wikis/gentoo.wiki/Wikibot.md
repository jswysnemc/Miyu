The Gentoo **wikibot** it a bot that sits in [[#gentoo-wiki-changes](ircs://irc.libera.chat/#gentoo-wiki-changes)] ([[webchat](https://web.libera.chat/#gentoo-wiki-changes)]) to report changes to users of the channel, in near real time.

## [Configuration]

[UTF-8](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/UTF-8 "UTF-8") (unicode) support is required for the bot output to display correctly. A font that supports UTF-8 \"[emoji](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/emoji#In_Unicode "wikipedia:emoji")\" characters, such as [[[media-fonts/noto-emoji]](https://packages.gentoo.org/packages/media-fonts/noto-emoji)[]], may also be required.

** Tip**\
With proper UTF-8 support and a compatible font, the bot should display properly even in terminal-based clients.

## [Usage]

The bot does not support any commands currently.
