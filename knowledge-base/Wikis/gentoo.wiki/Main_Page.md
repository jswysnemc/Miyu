# [][Welcome to the Gentoo wiki!]

Larry the Cow beckons you to explore the Gentoo wiki!

Spot a mistake, omission, broken link, etc.? Please [just fix it](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/What_to_do_when_noticing_an_error_on_the_wiki "What to do when noticing an error on the wiki"), it\'s easy\![ ]

Making a small change is simple: [once logged-in](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Special:CreateAccount "Special:CreateAccount"), click the *Edit* button at the top of a page or next to a section title, make the appropriate change to the text, add a brief summary stating *what* the edit changes and *why*, click the *Save changes* button - done!\

------------------------------------------------------------------------

For useful information on editing the wiki, see the quick-start guide on [what to do when noticing an error on the wiki‎](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/What_to_do_when_noticing_an_error_on_the_wiki "What to do when noticing an error on the wiki"), the [contributor\'s guide](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Contributor%27s_guide "Gentoo Wiki:Contributor's guide"), and the [guidelines](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Guidelines "Gentoo Wiki:Guidelines").

For the *occasional quick fix*, don\'t necessarily worry too much about [formatting and style](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Contributor%27s_guide "Gentoo Wiki:Contributor's guide") if it\'s too much - what\'s important is to fix errors or add missing information to the wiki.

If having any trouble getting started, there are often people willing to help in the [[#gentoo-wiki](ircs://irc.libera.chat/#gentoo-wiki)] ([[webchat](https://web.libera.chat/#gentoo-wiki)]) [IRC](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/IRC "IRC") channel. Please be patient, replies can take a very long time: be prepared to wait for several hours after asking a question.

Note that some articles are protected from public edits, such as the [handbook](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Handbook:Main_Page "Handbook:Main Page") and [project pages](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Project:Gentoo "Project:Gentoo"). Issues on these pages can still be [reported](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/What_to_do_when_noticing_an_error_on_the_wiki#Inform_editors_of_issues_on_protected_pages_and_propose_solutions "What to do when noticing an error on the wiki") though.

For anyone willing to help the Gentoo community out a little more than with the occasional quick fix, please do check out the [contributor\'s guide](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Contributor%27s_guide "Gentoo Wiki:Contributor's guide") and the [wiki editing guidelines](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Guidelines "Gentoo Wiki:Guidelines") to learn how to make quality edits. The [help improve Gentoo by getting involved with documentation!](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Help_improve_Gentoo_by_getting_involved_with_documentation! "Help improve Gentoo by getting involved with documentation!") page lists many of the ways wiki content can be improved.

## [ Available documentation]

To find information use the search box on the top right corner of the screen, or categorically browse the wiki using the [Documentation topic](#Documentation_topics) links provided below.

Both official Gentoo project documentation as well as community-contributed content can be found here. The vast majority of documentation is publicly editable. Official documents have a *Handbook:* or *Project:* prefix (only developers can edit them). Community members can leave comments/feedback for these pages via their respective *Discussion* pages.

Find something to add or edit? The [*Getting started*](#Getting_started) section (below) gives contributors a few pointers on how to start editing articles.

Most importantly, the **Gentoo Handbook** is among the best documents found on the wiki:

## [ News]

#### [][February 18, 2026: Wiki server overwhelmed by scrapers]

The wiki has been enduring heavy scraper bot activity recently, to the point that the site has been repeatedly overwhelmed. These overloads have been causing errors such as *500 Internal Server*, *Bad Gateway (502)*, *Gateway Timeout (504)*, or long or incomplete page loading. The [infrastructure](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Project:Infrastructure "Project:Infrastructure") team has been working to keep the servers running through all this, and hopefully the issues will be contained.

The core services powering Gentoo, connectivity, parts, repair, remote hands, hosting, and power all need to be paid for by Gentoo - any [donations](https://www.gentoo.org/donate/) to help keep the lights on are appreciated.

## [ Getting started]

Anyone is welcome to edit or create pages on this wiki.

Gentoo wiki contributors encourage beginners to consult the [Help page](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Help:Contents "Help:Contents") before making edits. It also links to the [MediaWiki User\'s Guide](https://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/Help:Contents) which contains information on how to use wiki software.

If there is something we can improve please let us know on the [Feedback page](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Gentoo_Wiki:Feedback "Gentoo Wiki:Feedback").

For questions *related to using or editing the documentation on the [wiki]*, please join us in our [[#gentoo-wiki](ircs://irc.libera.chat/#gentoo-wiki)] ([[webchat](https://web.libera.chat/#gentoo-wiki)]) IRC channel on Libera.chat or email us at [wiki@gentoo.org](mailto:wiki@gentoo.org).

**Please note**: For *general Gentoo support*, such as getting advice on a particular Handbook section or other generic support questions, please get in touch with our [support community](https://www.gentoo.org/support/) instead. General support is *[not]* provided by the wiki project.

** Note**\
Due to usability issues the [visual editor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:VisualEditor "wikipedia:Wikipedia:VisualEditor") is currently disabled. See [[[bug #910279]](https://bugs.gentoo.org/show_bug.cgi?id=910279)[]] for more details.
