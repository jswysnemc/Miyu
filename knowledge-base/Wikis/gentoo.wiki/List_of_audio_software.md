This page gives brief lists of packages related to playing music/audio, such as mixers / volume controls, players, editors, and so on.

For packages related to music production, refer to the \"[List of music production software](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/List_of_music_production_software "List of music production software")\" page.

For information about setting up audio output and input on Gentoo, refer to the [PipeWire](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/PipeWire "PipeWire"), [PulseAudio](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/PulseAudio "PulseAudio"), [ALSA](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/ALSA "ALSA") and [JACK](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/JACK "JACK") pages.

When adding entries::

-   The text of the \'Name\' column should link to, in order of priority:
    -   an existing page on the wiki for that software; or, if that does not yet exist,
    -   the homepage for that software, which can be found via e.g. `equery m `.

<!-- -->

-   The text of the \'Description\' column should be, in order of priority:
    -   the short description, e.g. `equery m -d `; or, if that isn\'t available,
    -   the description in the software\'s man page, together with a [\"Man\" template](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Template:Man "Template:Man") linking to that page; or, if that isn\'t available,
    -   to the official documentation.

[Equery](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Equery "Equery") is used in the above examples, but other ways of accessing that information are available, e.g via [Emerge](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Emerge "Emerge") or [eix](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Eix "Eix").
