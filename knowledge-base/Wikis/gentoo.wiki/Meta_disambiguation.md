Within the Gentoo project space, the term of \'meta\' is often used as an adjective or a prefix. It has different meanings depending on the word it augments.

Meanings include:

-   [Meta article](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Meta_article "Meta article") --- an article that provides a general overview or summary of conceptually similar ideas and related software available in Gentoo Linux.
-   [Meta ebuilds](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Meta_ebuilds "Meta ebuilds") --- an ebuild containing a developer-selected, curated software suite that generally relates to a certain *family* of related software packages.
-   [Metadistribution](https://wiki.gentoo.org/index.php?title=Metadistribution&action=edit&redlink=1 "Metadistribution (page does not exist)")
-   Metastructure - See the [Social Contract](https://www.gentoo.org/get-started/philosophy/social-contract.html) and [Metastructure Project](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Project:Metastructure "Project:Metastructure").
