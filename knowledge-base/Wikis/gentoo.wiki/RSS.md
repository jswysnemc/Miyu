## [Available software]

### [Feed readers]

Feed readers reside within the [net-news](https://packages.gentoo.org/categories/net-news) category. To list them, run:

`user `[`$`]`eix -cC net-news`

### [Web browser support]

[Pale Moon](https://wiki.gentoo.org/index.php?title=Pale_Moon&action=edit&redlink=1 "Pale Moon (page does not exist)") has native support for feed autodiscovery and listing. Other browsers need a third-party extension for that functionality.

### [Web applications]

Selfhosted news aggregation applications give such benefits as easy synchronization between devices or [email](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Email "Email") notifications.

-   [[[www-apps/nextcloud]](https://packages.gentoo.org/packages/www-apps/nextcloud)[]] with the [News](https://apps.nextcloud.com/apps/news) application installed
-   [[[www-apps/selfoss]](https://packages.gentoo.org/packages/www-apps/selfoss)[]]
-   [[[www-apps/tt-rss]](https://packages.gentoo.org/packages/www-apps/tt-rss)[]]
