On Saturday 30 January 2016 and Sunday 31 January 2016, the [Free and Open Source Software Developers\' European Meeting FOSDEM 2016](https://fosdem.org/2016/) will take place in Brussels, Belgium.

Naturally a Gentoo crowd will be there; this page is intended to collect public information about our presence and help organizing stuff.
