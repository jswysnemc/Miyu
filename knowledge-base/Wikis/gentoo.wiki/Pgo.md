Within the Gentoo project space, the term \"**pgo**\" can have several different meanings that include:

-   [packages.gentoo.org](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Packages.gentoo.org "Packages.gentoo.org") - website that provides information about packages available in the [Gentoo ebuild repository](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/Ebuild_repository#The_Gentoo_ebuild_repository "Ebuild repository") (*p.g.o*).
-   [Profile Guided Optimization](https://wiki.gentoo.org/wiki/GCC_optimization#Profile_Guided_Optimization_.28PGO.29 "GCC optimization") - Profile Guided Optimization (*PGO*).
