## Audio
Requires Sound Open Firmware to fully function.

## Backlight
Need manually configuring kernel parameter to functioning.

See Backlight#Kernel command-line options.

## Burning DVD DL
When burning DVD DL, growisofs may encounter errors. Use cdrecord instead, see Optical disc drive#BD-R DL 50GB errors on trying to burn second layer:

 $ cdrecord speed=2 dev=/dev/sr0 /path/to/iso
