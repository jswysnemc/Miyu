## Flash
Adobe Flash Player support ended on 31 December 2020. Most browsers also have stopped supporting plugins, relegating alternatives like  to only work standalone.

On modern browsers, Ruffle is a Flash Player emulator written in Rust, available through the use of WebAssembly. Unlike the defunct Flash Player plugin and Lightspark, Ruffle is only available in browsers as an extension (e.g. for Firefox or Chromium).

To run natively, Ruffle can be installed with one of the  or  packages.
