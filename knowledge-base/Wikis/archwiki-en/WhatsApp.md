# WhatsApp

WhatsApp is a mobile-first instant messaging service owned by Meta, featuring end-to-end encryption. It requires an active mobile phone number and data connection to operate.

There is no official WhatsApp desktop client for Linux, and Meta has strictly attempted to ban third-party clients and plugins using their protocol. You might want to avoid using WhatsApp altogether in favor of IM services with more openness, such as XMPP, , Telegram, or Matrix.
