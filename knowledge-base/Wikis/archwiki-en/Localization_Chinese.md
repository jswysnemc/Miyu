# Localization/Chinese

This article describes how to set up a Chinese language environment.

## Fonts
* adobe source han fonts 思源字体
**  - Simplified Chinese OpenType/CFF Sans fonts
**  - Traditional Chinese OpenType/CFF Sans fonts
**  - Hong Kong Chinese OpenType/CFF Sans fonts
**  - Simplified Chinese OpenType/CFF Serif fonts
**  - Traditional Chinese OpenType/CFF Serif fonts
**  - Hong Kong Chinese OpenType/CFF Serif fonts
* noto Chinese fonts (include adobe source han fonts and other fonts)
**  - Google Noto TTF fonts
**  - Google Noto CJK fonts
**  - Google Noto emoji fonts
**  - Noto CJK-SC fonts for Simplified Chinese
**  - Noto CJK-TC fonts for Traditional Chinese
* wqy fonts
**  - WenQuanYi Micro Hei font family (also known as Hei, Gothic or Dotum) is a sans-serif style derived from Droid Sans Fallback, it offers high quality CJK outline font and it is extremely compact (~5M).
**  - Hei Ti Style (sans-serif) Chinese Outline font embedded with bitmapped Song Ti (also supporting Japanese (partial) and Korean characters).
**  - Bitmapped Song Ti (serif) Chinese font.
* arphic fonts
**  - Kaiti (brush stroke) Unicode font (enabling anti-aliasing is suggested)
**  - Mingti (printed) Unicode font
*  - New Sung font, previously is ttf-fireflysung package
*  - Chinese and Vietnamese ttf fonts
* Standard fonts of Traditional Chinese
**  - Kai and Song traditional Chinese font from the Ministry of Education of Taiwan
**  Chinese TrueType fonts by Ministry of Education of Taiwan government, supporting CNS11643 standard, including Kai and Sung fontface.
* Windows Chinese fonts
**  - windows8 simple Chinese fonts.
**  - windows8 traditional Chinese fonts.
**  - windows10 simple Chinese fonts.
**  - windows10 traditional Chinese fonts.
**  - windows11 simple Chinese fonts.
**  - windows11 traditional Chinese fonts.
*  - CJK serif font that emphasis on an old-style typeface.
*  - Sarasa Gothic, which is a CJK monospaced font based on Iosevka and Source Han Sans.

* Founder Type fonts 方正字体

* MiSans fonts 小米字体
**
**  - MiSans TC covers Traditional Chinese
**  - MiSans L3 specifically covers Level-3 rare words (and does not include Level-1 and -2 characters)

* LINE Seed fonts
**  - LINE Seed TW font (OTF)
**  - LINE Seed TW font (TTF)
