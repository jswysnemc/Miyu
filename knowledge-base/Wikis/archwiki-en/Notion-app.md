## Tips and tricks
## Reset local cache
The official Notion wiki provides a guide for Windows and Mac, but not for Linux since only the web browser version is provided and no native version.

If you have to reset the locally cached Notion data because of technical bugs, delete all the content of the  folder.
On next startup of Notion, you will have to log in again. The data will be fetched fresh from Notion servers.
