##  字型

  * 思源体 
    * [adobe-source-han-sans-cn-fonts](<https://archlinux.org/packages/?name=adobe-source-han-sans-cn-fonts>)包——简体中文紧凑字型格式无衬线体
    * [adobe-source-han-sans-tw-fonts](<https://archlinux.org/packages/?name=adobe-source-han-sans-tw-fonts>)包——繁体中文紧凑字型格式无衬线体
    * [adobe-source-han-sans-hk-fonts](<https://archlinux.org/packages/?name=adobe-source-han-sans-hk-fonts>)包——香港中文紧凑字型格式无衬线体
    * [adobe-source-han-serif-cn-fonts](<https://archlinux.org/packages/?name=adobe-source-han-serif-cn-fonts>)包——简体中文紧凑字型格式衬线体
    * [adobe-source-han-serif-tw-fonts](<https://archlinux.org/packages/?name=adobe-source-han-serif-tw-fonts>)包——繁体中文紧凑字型格式衬线体
    * [adobe-source-han-serif-hk-fonts](<https://archlinux.org/packages/?name=adobe-source-han-serif-hk-fonts>)包——香港中文紧凑字型格式衬线体
  * noto 中文字型 
    * [noto-fonts-sc](<https://aur.archlinux.org/packages/noto-fonts-sc/>)AUR \- Noto CJK-SC 简体中文字型
    * [noto-fonts-tc](<https://aur.archlinux.org/packages/noto-fonts-tc/>)AUR \- Noto CJK-TC 繁体中文字型
  * 文泉驿字型 
    * [wqy-microhei](<https://archlinux.org/packages/?name=wqy-microhei>)包 \- WenQuanYi Micro Hei 字体（即 Hei，Gothic 或 Dotum）是一个由 Droid Sans Fallback 来的无衬线体轮廓字型，它有高质 CJK 字型且极度紧凑（～５Ｍ）。
    * [wqy-zenhei](<https://archlinux.org/packages/?name=wqy-zenhei>)包 \- 有宋体在内的黑体轮廓字型（有韩文和部分日文的支援）。
    * [wqy-bitmapfont](<https://archlinux.org/packages/?name=wqy-bitmapfont>)包 \- 点阵宋体字型。
  * arphic fonts 
    * [ttf-arphic-ukai](<https://archlinux.org/packages/?name=ttf-arphic-ukai>)包 \- _Kaiti_ (brush stroke) Unicode font (enabling anti-aliasing is suggested)
    * [ttf-arphic-uming](<https://archlinux.org/packages/?name=ttf-arphic-uming>)包 \- _Mingti_ (printed) Unicode font
  * [opendesktop-fonts](<https://archlinux.org/packages/?name=opendesktop-fonts>)包 \- _New Sung_ font, previously is ttf-fireflysung package
  * [ttf-hannom](<https://archlinux.org/packages/?name=ttf-hannom>)包 \- Chinese and Vietnamese ttf fonts
  * Standart fonts of the Republic of China ministry of education in Taiwan 
    * [ttf-tw](<https://aur.archlinux.org/packages/ttf-tw/>)AUR \- Kai and Song traditional Chinese font from the Ministry of Education of Taiwan
    * [ttf-twcns-fonts](<https://aur.archlinux.org/packages/ttf-twcns-fonts/>)AUR Chinese TrueType fonts by Ministry of Education of Taiwan government, support CNS11643 standard, including Kai and Sung fontface.
  * Windows Chinese fonts 
    * [ttf-ms-win8-zh_cn](<https://aur.archlinux.org/packages/ttf-ms-win8-zh_cn/>)AUR \- windows8 simple Chinese fonts.
    * [ttf-ms-win8-zh_tw](<https://aur.archlinux.org/packages/ttf-ms-win8-zh_tw/>)AUR \- windows8 traditional Chinese fonts.
    * [ttf-ms-win10-zh_cn](<https://aur.archlinux.org/packages/ttf-ms-win10-zh_cn/>)AUR \- windows10 simple Chinese fonts.
    * [ttf-ms-win10-zh_tw](<https://aur.archlinux.org/packages/ttf-ms-win10-zh_tw/>)AUR \- windows10 traditional Chinese fonts.
  * [ttf-i.bming](<https://aur.archlinux.org/packages/ttf-i.bming/>)AUR \- CJK serif font that emphasis on an old-style typeface.
